Project Idea

ToDoTopia

ToDoTopia is a gamified productivity system for teams and individuals. It gamifies the mundane tasks you must do every day. Whether you’re a university student or you work as an IT professional, this app will gamify your work life. When you’re a part of a team, you can build a village together by checking off tasks on your to-do list. When you’re ready to do a Sprint, you can raid another town and compete for the town of the week in your country (and even world-wide!). ToDoTopia allows you to link Google Drive documents to any task. This is especially helpful if there are multiple people working on the same task. Subtasks are also a feature, streamlining your teamwork.
